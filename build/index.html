At Gästehaus Rader, our love for the mountains goes hand in hand with our commitment to preserving them. We believe that true hospitality means taking responsibility for the environment that hosts us.

To protect the pristine alpine environment, we produce our own green energy via high-efficiency on-site solar panels. By harnessing the power of the Carinthian sun, we minimize our carbon footprint while providing you with a modern, guilt-free luxury experience.
